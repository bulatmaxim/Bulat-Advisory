01
Service

Financial Forecasting & Modeling

What it is

Rolling 12–18 month models covering P&L, cash flow, and headcount. Scenario planning built in. Board-ready output with documented assumptions.

Best for

Pre-board meeting prep, fundraising diligence, new product pricing decisions, burn and runway analysis.

What you get

  • A model you can update monthly without starting over
  • Board-ready output with clear, defensible assumptions
  • Variance tracking that explains why actuals diverged from plan — not just that they did
  • Runway visibility at all times, in any scenario
02
Service

Monthly Performance Review

What it is

Month-end close support, variance analysis, and a plain-language executive narrative delivered every month — so you're never scrambling before a board meeting.

Best for

Companies that have financial data but no regular cadence for reviewing it with rigor.

What you get

  • A consistent, clean reporting package delivered every month
  • A clear answer to the question: "How are we really doing?"
  • Flagged risks and opportunities, not just backward-looking numbers
  • Confidence walking into every board meeting and investor update
03
Service

Annual Budgeting & Planning

What it is

A full budget cycle from kickoff to board presentation. Cross-functional, structured, and repeatable. Usually six to eight weeks.

Best for

Companies entering their first structured budget cycle, or those looking to professionalize an ad-hoc process before investor scrutiny increases.

What you get

  • A budget your department heads helped build — so they'll actually own the targets
  • A clear link between your operating plan and your financial plan
  • A repeatable process you can run every year, not reinvent from scratch
  • Board-ready budget presentation with commentary
04
Service

Decision Support & Ad-Hoc Analysis

What it is

Financial analysis for the decision in front of you right now — pricing, hiring, market entry, cost reduction. Delivered when you need it.

Best for

Any high-stakes operational or strategic decision where gut feel isn't enough and time is short.

What you get

  • A clear financial frame for the decision, with assumptions made explicit
  • Scenario comparisons that show you the range of outcomes — not just the optimistic one
  • A rigorous foundation so you can make a better, faster call
  • Analysis that's built for a decision-maker, not for a spreadsheet competition
